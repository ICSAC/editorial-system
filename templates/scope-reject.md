Dear {{greeting}},

Thank you for your submission, "{{paper_title}}".

After review, the panel has determined that this work falls outside the institute's editorial scope. ICSAC's remit is complexity science, information theory, persistence dynamics, and adjacent methodology. Submissions whose claims, methods, or epistemic framing place them outside that remit are returned without a revise-and-resubmit invitation, as no revision within scope is possible.

This is not a judgment on the work's merit on its own terms. It is a statement that the institute is not the appropriate venue.

## About the review process

This decision was reached through ICSAC's open review process — a multi-reviewer panel with human curator oversight. The review criteria, including scope, are published openly at [icsacinstitute.org](https://icsacinstitute.org).

If you believe this scope determination is in error, contact info@icsacinstitute.org with specific objections and we will re-evaluate.
