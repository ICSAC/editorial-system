## About the review process

This review was produced through ICSAC's open review process — a multi-reviewer panel with AI tooling helping draft and structure each review. Final decisions rest with human curators. The review criteria — scope alignment, methodological transparency, internal consistency, citation integrity, novelty, and AI slop detection — are published openly at [icsacinstitute.org](https://icsacinstitute.org).

If you believe this review contains errors or mischaracterizes your work, contact info@icsacinstitute.org with specific objections and we will re-evaluate.
